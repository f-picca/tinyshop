LA Confidential

L.A. Confidential, the Book and the Movie, are two different animals and the movie (arguably an instant classic) is by far the better of the two; a testament to the value of talented editing---which is not just cutting, but cutting and stitching, turning something that was flabby and dull into something sharp and compelling.




The Illuminatus! Trilogy

The Illuminatus! Trilogy is a pretty weird book, even by postmodern conventions. The narrative jumps back and forth between 3rd person and any given character without warning. There are quite a few plot threads, and the transitions between these threads are practically nonexistent; nothing is really there to signify a change in scene, and sometimes one thread even jumps to another mid-sentence.
